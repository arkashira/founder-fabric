### How founder‑fabric Creates Value

1. **Signal Ingestion** – Pulls real‑time market signals from diverse sources into a unified vector store.  
2. **Idea Synthesis** – Uses LLMs to map pain points to concrete product hypotheses via 5‑Why chains.  
3. **Portfolio Guardrail** – Ensures each idea is unique by cross‑checking against the existing catalog.  
4. **Revenue Validation** – Executes quick willingness‑to‑pay surveys and outreach scripts to confirm market fit.  
5. **Exportable PRD** – Outputs a ready‑to‑use markdown PRD, user stories, and MVP scope for immediate action.  

By automating the entire ideation‑validation loop, founder‑fabric reduces the time and risk founders face when launching new products, enabling them to focus on execution rather than discovery.
